Good Physical Health and Mental Health

Good health--both physical and mental--for people around me.

Those people include:

  • My family and myself
  • My CSCI-E12 Professor and Staff
  • My CSCI-E12 Classmates

Tips to achieve Good Physical Health

  • Eat LESS at these places (yes I know it's easier said than done!):
    • McDonald's
    • Burger King
    • KFC
    • Popeye's
  • Eat here instead:
    • Subway
    • Panera Bread
  • Exercise more, and get better sleep

Tips to achieve Good Mental Health

  • Exercise more, and get better sleep -- great for both physical and mental health!
  • Avoid situations that create stress for ourselves
